    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to the most common questions about admissions, courses, and student life at our university.

    What is the application process for undergraduate programs?

    The application process includes submitting an online application, transcripts, and standardized test scores. You may also be required to attend an interview or provide letters of recommendation.

    What are the tuition fees for international students?

    International student tuition fees vary depending on the program. You can check the specific program’s fee structure on our website or contact the admissions office for further information.

    How can I apply for financial aid or scholarships?

    Financial aid and scholarships are available for both undergraduate and graduate students. You can apply through the university’s financial aid portal after receiving your admission offer.

    What programs are available for graduate students?

    We offer a wide range of graduate programs in fields such as business, engineering, computer science, and arts. Visit the graduate programs page for a full list of offerings and admission requirements.

    How do I register for classes once I’m accepted?

    After acceptance, you will be given access to the student portal where you can register for classes, view course schedules, and check registration deadlines.

    What resources are available for international students?

    We offer a variety of support services including visa assistance, English language workshops, and cultural exchange programs to help international students adjust to life on campus.
